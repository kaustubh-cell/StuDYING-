# stuDYING 💀

built this in a 20 hour manic sprint bc my brain was fried, i couldn't focus, and i needed to lock in fr. 

it's a chill, minimalist workspace that lives right in your browser. no messy tabs, no distractions, just pure productivity vibes. i was tweaking over my study habits so i literally coded an entire fake os to fix it. 

## what's in it rn
* **terminal:** fake command line just to feel like a hacker while u work tbh.
* **tasks:** simple todo list to get your life together.
* **focus:** pomodoro timer to grind out those study sessions without getting cooked.
* **breathe:** a breathing widget to help u calm down when the math ain't mathing.
* **market:** gamified my life. u do tasks and focus -> u get energy -> u spend energy to unlock the notes and calc apps. massive W.
* **notes & calc:** locked behind the market. u gotta earn them. 

## tech stack
vanilla html/css/js. rawdogged it. 

no react, no heavy frameworks, no crazy dependencies. just me, a text editor, and 20 hours of lost sleep to make the window dragging animation feel buttery smooth. 

also used the golden ratio (1.618) for the math behind the animations and window sizing bc aesthetics matter fr. 

## how to run
literally just double click `index.html` and open it in your browser. 

no `npm install` bs. no local servers. it just works out of the box. all your saves (tasks, unlocked apps, energy) stay in local storage so u don't lose your progress when u close the tab.
